Gesture_Recognition.py contains the entire code used for recognition

Requirements:
Python 2.7
Opencv2 version 2.4

Steps for running the code:
1) Open the script in terminal
2) Palce your hand covering alll the white dots in the image such that your palm covers the square grid and your middle and ring finger covers the vertical white dots
3) Press esc key on the video feed
4) Your hand would appear on the screen, and now you can see that the gestures are being detected (five fingers, four fingers, three fingers, two fingers, horizontal motion, vertical motion)

Precautions:
Your webcam should not be tilted
Background should not be the same colour as your hand
